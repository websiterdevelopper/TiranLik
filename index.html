Our mission

Simplify Access to Sports Facilities

Our mission is to centralize all available sports courts and facilities in Casablanca on a single digital platform. By enabling fast discovery and real-time booking, TIRANLIK removes the friction of phone calls, informal coordination, and scheduling conflicts, making sports access simple, efficient, and reliable.

Promote Regular and Social Sports Practice

TIRANLIK aims to remove logistical barriers that limit sports participation in urban environments. Through a seamless, transparent, and user-friendly booking experience, the platform encourages young people, students, and amateur players to play more often, organize games easily, and strengthen social connections through sport.

Empower and Digitize Local Sports Providers

We support sports facility owners and managers in their digital transformation by offering increased visibility, smart management tools, and an optimized reservation system. TIRANLIK helps structure the local sports ecosystem while improving operational efficiency and revenue opportunities for venues.
